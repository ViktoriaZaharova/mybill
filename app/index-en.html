Who will our service suit?

Usually our services are used by banks, insurance companies and large retail. However, we have experience in preparing and launching projects for various companies.

Case 1

Objective: To increase transactions of bank cardholders.

Solution: We award points to the cardholder according to set rules based on the transactions made. The points can be exchanged for prizes, which we will deliver promptly.

Case 2

Objective: To increase sales of the product at a given point in time.

Solution: You place activation codes on the product. We award points at the time of code registration to the participants of the promotion. They exchange them for prizes.

Case 3

Objective: To attract new customers.

Solution: We award points to new customers for their first purchase according to certain rules based on your report. The new customers exchange the points for the prizes we deliver.
